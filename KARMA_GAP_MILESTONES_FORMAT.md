## Tips for Filling the Form

1. **Start with Milestone 1** - Click "Add Milestone" and fill in the first one
2. **End dates are required** - Make sure to set realistic end dates
3. **Add all 6 milestones** - Click "Add Milestone" after each one to add the next
4. **Use consistent dates** - Make sure milestones flow chronologically
5. **Save each milestone** - Click "Save Milestone" before adding the next one

---

*Fill in the dates based on your grant timeline (typically 6 months from grant start date)*
