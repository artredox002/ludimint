# LUDIMINT Pitch Deck - Presentation Guide

## How to Use This Pitch Deck

This pitch deck is designed to be flexible and adaptable for different audiences and presentation formats.

### Presentation Formats

#### 1. **PowerPoint/Google Slides**
- Convert each slide section into individual slides
- Use the markdown headings as slide titles
- Add visuals: charts, diagrams, screenshots
- Recommended: 16:9 aspect ratio

#### 2. **PDF Export**
- Export markdown to PDF using tools like Pandoc or Markdown to PDF converters
- Maintain slide breaks for clean presentation
- Add page numbers and branding

#### 3. **Live Presentation**
- Use slide numbers as talking points
- Allocate 1-2 minutes per slide
- Total presentation time: 15-20 minutes
- Leave 5-10 minutes for Q&A

### Customization Tips

#### **For Investors**
- Emphasize: Financial projections, business model, market opportunity, ROI potential
- De-emphasize: Technical details (unless technical investors)

#### **For Grant Applications**
- Emphasize: Social impact, alignment with mission, technical achievements, global reach
- De-emphasize: Revenue projections, business model

#### **For Partners**
- Emphasize: Integration opportunities, mutual benefits, market access
- De-emphasize: Financial details

#### **For Communities**
- Emphasize: User benefits, ease of use, fairness, accessibility
- De-emphasize: Business model, financials

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How do you prevent cheating?**
A: Commit-reveal cryptographic pattern ensures scores are hidden until reveal phase, preventing players from seeing others' scores before submitting.

**Q: What if MiniPay changes?**
A: Our architecture is flexible and can adapt to wallet changes. We're committed to Celo ecosystem.

**Q: How do you acquire users?**
A: Community partnerships, Celo ecosystem integration, social media marketing, and word-of-mouth growth.

**Q: What's your competitive moat?**
A: First-mover advantage, technical expertise, Celo ecosystem integration, and lowest fees in market.

**Q: How do you make money?**
A: Small platform fee (2-5%), premium features, and partnerships. Sustainable and transparent.
