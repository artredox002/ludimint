# Karma Gap Milestones - Celo Proof of Ship Grant

## Recommended Milestones (Add These)

### Milestone 1: Beta Launch & Initial User Acquisition
**Goal**: Launch beta version and acquire first active users
**Target**: 
- Launch public beta of LUDIMINT platform
- Onboard 100+ active users
- Host 50+ micro-tournaments
- Achieve $1,000+ in tournament volume

**Timeline**: Month 1-2

---

### Milestone 2: Community Building & Engagement
**Goal**: Build active community and establish partnerships
**Target**:
- Partner with 3+ gaming communities or Celo ecosystem projects
- Establish Discord/Telegram community with 500+ members
- Launch community-driven tournaments
- Achieve 70%+ user retention rate

**Timeline**: Month 2-3

---

### Milestone 3: User Growth & Platform Optimization
**Goal**: Scale user base and improve platform performance
**Target**:
- Reach 1,000+ active users
- Host 500+ tournaments total
- Achieve $10,000+ cumulative tournament volume
- Optimize mobile PWA performance (<3s load time)

**Timeline**: Month 3-4

---

### Milestone 4: Feature Enhancement & Multi-Game Support
**Goal**: Expand platform capabilities and game integrations
**Target**:
- Launch tournament creator tools for community organizers
- Integrate 2+ additional skill-based games
- Implement advanced analytics dashboard
- Achieve 5,000+ total tournament participants

**Timeline**: Month 4-5

---

### Milestone 5: Emerging Markets Expansion
**Goal**: Focus on financial inclusion in target regions
**Target**:
- Launch localized campaigns in 2+ emerging markets (Africa, LATAM, or Southeast Asia)
- Achieve 30%+ of users from emerging markets
- Process $25,000+ in tournament volume
- Collect and publish user impact stories

**Timeline**: Month 5-6

---

### Milestone 6: Platform Sustainability & Growth Metrics
**Goal**: Demonstrate sustainable growth and impact
**Target**:
- Reach 2,500+ active users
- Host 1,000+ tournaments total
- Achieve $50,000+ cumulative tournament volume
- Maintain <0.1% platform fee (lowest in market)
- Publish impact report with user testimonials

**Timeline**: Month 6
